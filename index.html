How It Works

Submitting and tracking your complaint is simple and straightforward.

1

Register & Login

Create your account using your UPSA student ID and institutional email to verify your identity.

2

Submit Complaint

Select a category, describe your issue in detail, and submit. You'll receive a unique reference number.

3

Track & Resolve

Monitor your complaint status in real-time through your dashboard and receive updates as it progresses.
